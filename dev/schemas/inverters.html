Inverters Schema

schemas/inverters

Inverter settings used for stability analysis via PowerModelsStability.

The following properties will be familiar for existing generator objects

  • pg, pg_ub, and pg_lb are vectors real power injection settings, setpoint, upper bound, and lower bound, respectively, of the inverter object,
  • qg, qg_ub, and qg_lb are vectors reactive power injection settings, setpoint, upper bound, and lower bound, respectively, of the inverter object
  • vg is the voltage setpoint of the inverter object

The following are specific to inverters,

  • mp is the $P-f$ droop coefficient at the inverter
  • mq is the $Q-V$ droop coefficient at the inverter
  • tau is the time constant of the measurement filter
  • r and x are internal resistance/reactance of the inverter object

The following are global settings

  • omega0 is the frequency setpoint
  • rN is the virtual resistor resistance, usually a large number
